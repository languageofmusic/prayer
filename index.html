Morning, October 25th, 2024

God, this is my way of talking to you. I know it's weird. You know it's weird. You know me. You know that I had the idea for this last night, and I can't resist doing it first thing in the morning.

This is my problem. I can't resist myself. My brain pulls me forward all day, in constant action. But sometimes it pulls me toward things I don't want to do. Things you told me not to do.

It would be better for me to do nothing. But I don't know how to do nothing. I take my phone to the bathroom because I can't stand to sit on the toilet without being entertained.

Please, help me rest. Help me rest in you. You are with me in every moment. Help me find joy in your presence. In the name of Jesus.
